Accelerate Your English from A1 to A2

Welcome to AuraESL! This classroom-optimized single page application is built to guide students from beginner (A1) to elementary (A2) level in 30 minutes of interactive drills. Learn key grammar rules, vocabulary flashcards, speaking practice, and dedicated If-Clause Type 0 & 1 lessons.

🎯
👩‍🏫 Classroom Guide for Teachers:
  • This app is optimized for projector screens. Use the A+ / A- text options to make it visible from the back row.
  • Switch to Light Theme if the projector contrast is low.
  • Encourage students to click vocabulary cards to flip them. Use the Speaking Drills for active classroom pair-work!
